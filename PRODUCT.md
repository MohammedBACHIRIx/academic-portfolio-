# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Academic peers, researchers, university institutions, potential collaborators, and industry recruiters looking for expertise in microgrids and power electronics.

## Product Purpose
To serve as a professional academic portfolio for Mohammed Bachiri, showcasing his research publications, projects (like MG-FARM), and expertise in energy management systems and hardware-in-the-loop (HIL) simulations.

## Brand Personality
Professional, rigorous, forward-thinking, clear, and academic.

## Anti-references
Generic tech SaaS landing pages, overly flashy or trendy e-commerce templates, "startup" aesthetics.

## Design Principles
- Clarity and Readability: Research topics must be easily digestible.
- Academic Rigor: The design should reflect the seriousness of the research domain without being boring.
- Sustainable Motif: Subtle nods to renewable energy and efficiency.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Standard WCAG AA compliance, emphasizing strong contrast for readability of text-heavy publication descriptions.
